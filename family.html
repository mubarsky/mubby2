Family, children, and relationships

Ronaldo has five living children. He first became a father to a son, who was born on 17 June 2010 in the United States.[405] He has full custody of the child and has not publicly revealed the identity of the mother per an agreement with her.[406][407] In January 2015, Ronaldo's five-year relationship with Russian model Irina Shayk ended.[408]
